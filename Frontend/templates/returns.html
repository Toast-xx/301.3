Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase! If you're not completely satisfied, you can return or exchange your items easily. See below for our return and exchange policy details:

  • Return Period
    • Items can be returned or exchanged within 30 days of delivery.
  • Condition
    • Items must be unworn, in original packaging, and with all tags attached.
    • Footwear must be tried on indoors only.
  • How to Return
    • Print your prepaid shipping label and drop off at any courier location.
    • Exchanges can be requested for a different size or color (subject to availability).
  • Refunds
    • Refunds are processed within 5 business days after we receive your return.
    • Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
    • Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
  • Return Shipping
    • Returns are free for all domestic orders using our prepaid label.
    • International customers are responsible for return shipping costs.
Note: Final sale items, gift cards, and worn merchandise cannot be returned or exchanged.
Order Issues: If you received a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery for a free replacement or refund.
Need help?

Contact our Customer Service for assistance with your return or exchange.

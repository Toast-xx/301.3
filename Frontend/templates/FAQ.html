Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I place an order?
    • Browse our products, select your size and quantity, then click "Add to Cart." Proceed to checkout to complete your purchase.
  • What payment methods do you accept?
    • We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Stripe payments.
  • How can I track my order?
    • Order status tracking is coming soon!
  • What are your shipping options?
    • We offer Free Shipping, Standard ($5), and Express ($15) options. See our Shipping Info page for details.
  • How do I return or exchange an item?
    • Items must be unworn and returned within 30 days. See our Returns Policy for more info.
  • How do I know which size to order?
    • Check our Size Guide for detailed sizing charts and tips.
  • Can I change or cancel my order?
    • If your order hasn't shipped, contact us as soon as possible at support@shoehaven.com or use Live Chat.
  • Do you ship internationally?
    • Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout.
  • How do I contact customer service?
    • Email us at support@shoehaven.com or use our Contact Us page or Live Chat for immediate help.
